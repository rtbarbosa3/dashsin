Day Sat 09 May 2026 · peak 18:27
Highlights · operational day

SIN load averaged 78,382 MWmed with a peak of 92,483 MW at 18:27. Renewables (wind + solar + hydro + Itaipu) covered 90.1% of generation; thermal share was only 7.4% with an additional 2.6% from nuclear.

Wind/solar curtailment in NE peaked at 2,096 MW — driven mostly by transmission constraints (62% RFB) and reliability margin (28% CNF). Even with NE EAR at 95% and surplus inflow, the NE→SE/CO transmission cap means renewable surplus has to be cut at source.

Thermal dispatch was 5,776 MWmed, dominated by INF (inflexibility) at 3,425 MWmed — must-run plants regardless of price. Discretionary dispatch (OME/FOM) was 1,837 MWmed, mostly in SE/CO. 11 UTEs remain with suspended operation by recent ANEEL orders.
