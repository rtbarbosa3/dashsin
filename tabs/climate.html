Snapshot May 2026 · ENSO: weak El Niño
Highlights · climate state

Weak El Niño peaking and weakening — ONI index hit +0.7 in Jan-Feb 2026, now retreating to +0.4. NOAA forecasts ENSO-neutral conditions returning by Aug-Sep 2026. Historically, El Niño years bring above-average precipitation to Sul Brazil and below-average to Norte/Nordeste — consistent with what we see today.

Iguaçu/Uruguai basins at 140% MLT for May — the wettest May in Sul Brazil since 2022. Paraná basin at 82% MLT (slightly below normal), São Francisco closing out wet season at 95% MLT, Tocantins at 65% MLT after a strong Q1.

Mato Grosso do Sul and Goiás below 80% MLT — concerning for safrinha de milho. RS at 145% and PR at 130% of MLT carries the opposite risk: excess rain delays trigo plantio and damages final-stage soja. Bahia algodão zone at 105% — favorable.
